About Adam

About the Designer

Adam Gottlieb, owner of Gottlieb Landscape Design, received his Bachelor of Science in Landscape Architecture (B.S.L.A.) from the University of Wisconsin in 1998.

 Adam’s certifications include…

  •  LakeSmart Certified (Dept. of Environmental Protection)

  • I.C.P.A. Certified (Interlocking Concrete Paver Institute)

  • N.C.M.A. Certified (National Concrete and Masonry Association)

  • Certified Landscape Technician

  • B.S. Landscape Architecture

Since childhood, Adam has loved observing living things and has been inspired by his surroundings. He was influenced by the strong women in his family who had flourishing gardens and took great care of their surroundings. Guided by his Grandmother and Aunt, both respected landscape designers, Adam absorbed their horticultural knowledge for the region.

Over the past fifteen years, Adam has gained respect and experience in the green industry as he continues his commitment and further study in landscape design. He is constantly challenging himself and looking for ways to grow with the changing environment and needs of his clients.

Adam’s enthusiasm shines through from site analysis to crafting a plan that exceeds his clients visions. Adam provides the personal attention needed to transform the outside of your home. With fine craftsmanship and a natural ability to nourish the land as he works, Adam will design and install an outdoor space that will enhance the lives of its owners.

Gottlieb Landscape Design is a fully insured company.
